About Find my School

Looking for the best schools nearby?

This project is aimed at making the admission process hassle free and seamless which saves a lot of botheration and uncententy while giving you the assurance and acknowledgement instantly the moment you fill up your credentials step by step wiz the TC marksheets, id proofs, photographs and after the successful completion of the process you are taken to a gateway of payment (if any) and if the process is complete you are allotted a token/id and you are intimated/posted of your admission updates periodically. It gives you the freedom to apply in all eligible colleges and schools spread in nearby locality and far of globe who are members of this application . The commercial viability of this application all the parents and the students in fact are in dire need of a universal app which facilitates them to have access to the nearby and at the far end of college and university at a global extent . This app may become most sought after by all the educational institutions/universities because it gives them access to the universal student and parent fraternity .
